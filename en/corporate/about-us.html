About Us

Specialized In “Analytical Solutions” and “Sectoral Solutions“, OBASE is a Software Development Company, Information Technology Solution Provider and System Integrator.

Founded in 1995 OBASE is Turkey’s leading software and consulting company.

OBASE has developed innovative projects and software products under the status of R&D Center affiliated to the Ministry of Industry and Technology received academic and international awards.

It is one of  the top 20 local software producers in Turkey and 1st in Data Warehousing and Business Intelligence companies.

Based in Washington DC and Istanbul, OBASE helps national and international businesses with its 230+ highly talented team members.
