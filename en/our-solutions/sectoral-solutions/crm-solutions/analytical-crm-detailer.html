Detailer Analytical CRM

Predict Customer Behavior to Build Outstanding Relationships

Be Empowered to Maintain and Enrich Profitable Relationships

Obase Analytical CRM solution helps retailers gain a deeper knowledge of their customer base. With an omnichannel approach, the solution helps businesses better predict consumer behaviors and the shifts in consumer interests. This, in turn, enables companies to maintain better customer relationships and improve profitability.


From an increased ability to manage customer churns and understanding the reasons behind those churns, the solution also enables organizations to effectively anticipate and manage risks.

Trend Detection & Prediction
Ability to Better Manage Customer Churn
Improvement in Company’s Bottom Line
Enrichment in Profitable Relationships
Highly Customized Campaigns
Improved Customer Relationships

Obase brings together retail adaptations of models such as customer segmentation, basket and RFM analysis, and segment transition scores to deliver a well-structured system for the CRM managers. With the data cleansing and deduplication functionalities, the customer information base is always kept updated and of good quality.


The behaviors of the target and control groups are measured and the success of the campaigns are evaluated with the Campaign Performance Analysis. 

  • 24% Increase ROI of Campaigns
  • 30% savings on campaign budget
  • Track & Improve your marketing performance
  • Increase Revenue and Customer Retention
