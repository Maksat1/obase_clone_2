Комплект электронной трансформации

OBASE предоставляет компаниям provides companies with process management, document management and e-transformation solutions within the framework of digital asset management.

Budget and environment-friendly e-transformation solutions automate the operational business processes of companies and allow easy management for these processes from a single center. E-transformation solutions in compliance with the legislation and new generation architecture have been developed to help companies save money by reducing maintenance costs.


E-Transformation Suite Solutions:


E-Waybill

OBASE OS Connector supports the e-Waybill process in full compliance with the legal regulations published by the Revenue Administration. Erp is a part of the digitalization process with operational efficiency, record security and cost savings. Archiving service is also offered.


E-invoice

OBASE OS Connector supports the e-invoice process in full compliance with the legal regulations published by the Revenue Administration. It is part of the digitization process with operational efficiency, record security and cost savings. It supports fast invoicing and closing process in the operational system. Erp works independently. Archiving service is also offered.


E-Ledger

With the OBASE E-Ledger Ox Connector product, the books that are required to be kept in accordance with the provisions of the Tax Procedure Law and the Turkish Commercial Code are prepared and recorded in electronic file format in accordance with the format and standards announced by the GİB, and the ledger report is sent to the GİB. It works in full compliance by following the legislation change published by the Revenue Administration. It facilitates e-ledger uploads with its flexible integration methods (xml, excel, api, direct database etc.). Erp works independently. Archiving service is also offered.


E-Archive

It works fully integrated with Edoksis. It supports the e-archive process in full compliance with the legal regulations published by the Revenue Administration. Erp is dependent.


E-Zrapor

It works fully integrated with Edoksis. It provides reporting of old generation payment recorder devices to GIb. Erp is dependent.


E-Pos Reconciliation

Integration with Netbt ensures that Store POS transactions are accounted by transferring them with Store sales transactions. Erp is addicted.


E-Current Reconciliation

By establishing integration with Edoksis, current balance, Ba-Bs reconciliations are made. Erp is dependent.
